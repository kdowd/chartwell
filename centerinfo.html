How to get here
We are located at 201 Hukanui Road, Hamilton.
Travelling by Car

If you are coming from the North, drive down State Highway 1 towards Hamilton until you reach the first roundabout, you will see Burger King opposite on the right. Turn left onto Wairere Drive and continue down to the fourth roundabout. At this roundabout turn right onto Hukanui Road. You will find us 1km down Hukanui Road. We have heaps of parking.

If you are coming from the South, drive down State Highway 1 until you reach the first roundabout and turn left onto Cobham Drive. Continue along Cobham Drive until you reach Galloway Street on your right. Turn right onto Galloway and stay on this road, it will turn into Peachgrove Road and then Hukanui Road. You will find us 6km up this road on your left. We have heaps of parking.

Public Transport

We are lucky to have an excellent bus service to and from the City Transport Centre in Ward Street, Hamilton. This service is run by the Waikato Regional Council. The routes servicing Chartwell are the 4, 5, 16, 14, RDW as well as the Orbiter. For more information and timetables call Busline on (07) 856 4579 or visit busit.co.nz.

Taxi
Taxis are available for travel to and from Chartwell. Call Hamilton Taxi Society on 0800 477 477
Personal sessions
Because we like to look after our customers we have free customer car parking for up to four hours (P240) in the majority of our Centre car parks. There are also one and a half hour (P90) parking zones for those looking to make a more express visit. Look out for the markings to make sure you park in the right space.
Free extended parking
If you really want to make a day of it and think you will be visiting us for more that 4 hours then please visit the Customer Service Desk on Level 2. The kind staff there will give you an extended parking coupon which will last for the whole day. You will need to display this on your dashboard. Remember, car parks are for the use of Chartwell customers only, so please don't be offended if you are asked to verify that you have been shopping at the Centre.
Parking with prams
If you are shopping with young children we have designated some specially marked Parents with Prams parking spots located at the entrances of the centre.

Styling sessions
Do you know what works for your shape, size and colouring? With new collections coming out all the time, knowing what trends to follow can be tricky. But, with the help of one our expert personal stylists you will look and feel great in no time. Sit down with one our stylists and work out the best fit for you. Our experts will guide you on the best colours for your tones, the best shape for you body and the best budget for your wallet!  With their trained style eye and a collection of the latest fashion at their fingertips, they will arm you with all the tools to confidently select next season’s wardrobe. Personal Styling Sessions are available Monday to Sunday during normal shopping hours all year round. Prices start from $230 for two hours.
Group sessions

It’s time for a girl’s shopping trip! Grab a friend or two and come along to a Group Styling Session with one of our expert stylists. Our Group Styling Sessions are a fun way to shop with friends and designed for groups of two to four people. In just two to three hours our expert stylist will assess your body shape, style, colouring and budget, then take you to the right stores to try on the right pieces. After a session you and your friends will be armed with all the skills to build a perfect wardrobe. Group Styling Sessions are available Monday to Sunday during normal shopping hours all year round.

Prices start from $320 for two hours for two people.

Mobility and wheelchair hire
For those customers requiring assistance around our centre we have wheelchairs and mobility scooters available for hire. Just visit the Customer Services Desk on Level 2 for more information. There is no charge for hireage but you will require photo ID and terms and conditions apply.
Shopping with children
Parents room
Relax in private in our Parents Room, specially designed to make shopping with little ones easier. We have two parent spaces, each equipped with a microwave, bottle warmer, change tables and kids toilets, all in a safe and comfortable environment. Anyone looking after small children is welcome. You will find a Parents Room on Level 2 next to Whitcoulls and one on Level 1 near Rodney Wayne.

For more information please see the Customer Service Desk or call us on 07 853 0507.

Kids play area

Kids love to play and we have the perfect play area for our visitors under 5. If they need some down time from the shops head along to our play area in the foodcourt on Level 2 outside EVENT Cinemas. You must supervise your children as Chartwell Shopping Centre does not provide supervision. To keep our play area safe and clean we ask that kids please remove their shoes and have no food or drink in this area. Chartwell takes no responsibility for any injury from the use of the play area. Any use of this area by children outside of age limit is deemed unauthorised use.

Stroller and Kiddie Kruzer hire

If you forgot the stroller or someone’s legs are getting too tired, we have some wheels available to hire. You can pick up a stroller from our Customer Service Desk. There is no charge, however photo ID is required and terms and conditions apply.

Kiddie Kruzzers are also available for hire from the Customer Service Desk. We have single and double seating models, and trust us, the Kiddie Kruzzer is the coolest way to get around! An hourly charge applies for Kruzzers—$5 per single, $8 per double—and photo ID is required.
Mummy Mornings
